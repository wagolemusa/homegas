Kitchen & Cooking

You don’t have to be a professional chef or a gourmet cook to appreciate propane’s fast and precise temperature control. Even baking in a gas oven has a huge advantage over electric as the even heat is moist and prohibits the scorching and cracking of foods. Plus cooking with gas can save a homeowner 20% to 35% on their electric consumption for cooking. Perhaps best of all – a gas-powered range still works in a power outage.
Home Gas offers a vast array of ranges and cooktops and will help customers select the perfect appliance for their kitchen. In today’s world of technology we’re happy to also search online for any appliances that we currently do not have in stock that suits you. Once the ideal appliance is determined we will order that model and have it brought in for you.
